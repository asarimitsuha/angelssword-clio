The World of Mirane

A settlement born of defiance, clinging to warmth at the edge of an unforgiving tundra.

Welcome to Mirane, which stands as a newly founded settlement on the edge of the Mirane Tundra—an area impossible to traverse by all but the most seasoned adventurers. Yet the writings of the tundra's namesake herself spoke of a hidden, lush valley and, against all odds, these writings proved true. Warmed by geothermal forces, the valley is livable, though still surrounded by a frigid desert as perilous as any war-torn battleground. Isolation is both a gift and a curse to this newly formed town.

Despite that barrier of the frozen wasteland, Mirane's proximity to the Astra Line drew people from every corner of the Lyrian Peninsula. This oasis in the polar wastes has become a beacon for wanderers, scholars, and fortune-seekers, all flocking to test their fate at the edge of a realm shrouded in myth and menace. Though the settlement itself remains free from the eerie miasma known to corrupt flesh and mind, it stands ever watchful over the Astra Line beyond.

✦ ✦ ✦

The Astra Line is a cursed territory, where those without the strength of unwavering souls are lost to the malignant force—a darkness that twists life into fiends and spreads its vile influence across forests and ruins alike. The Adventurer's Association forbids entry into this zone except for the highest-ranked expeditions and skybound adventurers, and even then, only under strict sanction. But Mirane lies outside of any established nation's borders, granting a dangerous sort of freedom to those bold enough to push the limits of fate.

In this precarious frontier, Mirane serves as a launch point for foragers who seek rare materials, researchers who chase new remedies, and adventurers who yearn for the unknown. It is a testament to mankind's spirit of exploration, where each day offers the possibility of untold riches—or unspeakable horrors—lurking just beyond the veil of ice.
