Welcome to the World of Lyr

A vast world of high action anime fantasy — from bustling trade cities to frozen alpine frontiers, ancient alchemical academies to fiend-haunted wilds. Countless adventures, factions, and stories await across the lands of Lyr.

⚔️

High Action Anime Fantasy

A vast world with intense combat, airship battles, expeditions into fiend territory, and high-stakes adventures — all rendered in gorgeous anime style.

🧪

Alchemy & Magic

Brew potions, transmute materials, and weave spells. The magic system draws from deep alchemical lore woven into every aspect of the world.

🧝

Diverse Races

Play as Fairies, Chimerae, Elves, and more. Each race brings unique abilities, cultural depth, and story hooks to your adventures.

📖

Living Story

Born from a decade-long homebrew campaign, The Chronicles of Lyr is a living world with frequent updates and evolving narrative arcs.

⚔ Core System

Tactical Combat That Rewards Creativity

A deep, grid-based combat system with light attacks, heavy strikes, dodge rolls, blocks, and cover mechanics. Every decision in battle matters — position, timing, and teamwork determine victory.
